About the AICTE VAANI Scheme


AICTE's initiative to enhance technical education through vernacular languages, empowering students in their native tongue.

About the Workshop

The objective of this Workshop on “Next-Gen Security: Cryptography and Network Security” is to provide participants with a deep understanding of the latest advancements in Cryptography and Network Security, and to enable hands-on experience with security tools. After completing the Workshop, participants will be equipped to contribute to the research, development, and implementation of cutting-edge technologies in Cryptography and Network Security, which is an emerging field in the area of Information and Communication Technology (ICT).

Participants will gain hands-on experience through practical sessions, live demonstrations, and interactive discussions with industry experts.

About the Institute

J.J. College of Engineering & Technology

JJCET is a premier engineering institution located in Trichy, Tamil Nadu. Established with a vision to provide quality technical education, the college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines.

The institution is approved by AICTE and affiliated with Anna University, Chennai. JJCET is committed to excellence in technical education with state-of-the-art infrastructure.

About the Department

Department of Computer Science & Engineering

The Department of CSE at JJCET is committed to producing highly skilled computer professionals through quality education and research. The department offers B.E. in Computer Science and Engineering.

The department has well-equipped labs with modern computing facilities and experienced faculty members who are actively involved in research and consultancy.

The department has a strong track record of placements in leading IT companies.

Contents to be Covered in Workshop


Security Fundamentals

Security Trends, Attacks, Mechanisms and Services

Mathematical Foundations

GCD, Modular Arithmetic, Fermat & Euler Theorems, Chinese Remainder Theorem

Classical Encryption

Classical Encryption Techniques, Groups, Rings, Fields

Encryption Techniques

Substitution & Transposition Techniques, Primality Testing, Finite Fields, Discrete Logarithms

Block Ciphers

DES, Triple DES, AES, RC5

Public Key Cryptography

RSA, Diffie-Hellman Key Exchange

Elliptic Curve Cryptography

ECC Principles and Applications

Hash Functions & MAC

Hash Functions, MAC, MD5, SHA, HMAC, CMAC

Digital Signatures

Digital Signatures, DSS, ElGamal, Schnorr

Authentication Protocols

Authentication Protocols, Kerberos, X.509

IP Security & PKI

IP Security, IPv6, IKE, PKI

Secure Transactions

SSL/TLS, Secure Electronic Transactions, Recent Trends in Cryptography and Network Security
